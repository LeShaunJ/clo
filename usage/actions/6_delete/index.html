    Delete

    Deletes the records from the current model.

    clo delete clo delete --ids NUM [NUM ...] [--help]

    Options

    ‑‑ids NUM [NUM ...]

    ‑i NUM [NUM ...]

    (REQUIRED) The ID number(s) of the record(s) to perform the action on. Specifying - expects a space-separated list from STDIN.

    Peripherals

    Supportive, non-operational functions.

    ‑‑help

    ‑h

    Show this help message and exit.
